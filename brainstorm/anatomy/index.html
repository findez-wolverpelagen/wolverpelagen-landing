What we are

Not a seat company. Not a defense contractor. Not a software house.

Wolverpelagén builds the systems that keep the human at the helm effective, protected, and honoured through the rough sea — and the intelligence that learns from every mission.

We are an operator performance & protection company. We are vertically integrated by choice and by necessity — we design it, we build it, and in time we sell the intelligence itself. Everything resolves around one figure: the operator. The whole product line answers three questions about that person.

The Station

What the operator commands from. Seat, suspension, pedestal, modules. Jockey · Commander.

The Pelt

What the operator wears. Worn protection and load-bearing kit. The Pelt · Helmet.

The Mind

What coaches the operator. The Sideline coach and the data flywheel behind it. Cerebellum.

What we are trying to achieve

The goals, by horizon

A pre-project startup that writes one flat goal list drifts. We hold three horizons at once, and the nearest one is the only one that can kill us.

Near — Prove it exists

  • Working prototypes that can be shown and defended, not just described.
  • Governance closed — founder agreement signed, entity established, capital moving cleanly. This is the skull. Nothing below is safe until it exists.
  • First contract won or firmly in hand.

Mid — Prove it repeats

  • Certification pathway walked, not just mapped.
  • The manufacturing loop repeats — prototype to small batch without heroics.
  • First real mission data flowing — the flywheel starts to turn.

Long — Become the standard

  • The default operator system for the institutions we serve.
  • The intelligence sold as a platform, not only used in-house.
  • The natural evolution into unmanned systems and the wider horizon.

How we pay

Compensation philosophy

Because we hire only A-players and stay compact, we can afford to pay each of them well — and we should. The point is not to be cheap; the point is to be small, excellent, and generous to the few who carry the weight.

A-players only

We would rather have a seat empty than filled by someone who dilutes the pack.

Compact by design

Multiple seats per person is standard. Fewer people, each doing more, paid more.

Well paid

Compensation aims above the local market for the calibre we demand — cash, plus appreciation, financial and not.

Progressive

Pay grows with contribution and ownership. The path from Academy stipend to full seat is real and visible.

To be filled in together

Actual bands, equity, allowances, and contract types (PKWT, BPJS, stipends) are left blank on purpose — they get set against real runway once the entity and founder agreement are closed. This document defines the shape; the numbers come after the skull is set.

How a person becomes one of us

Wolver Academy

We do not hire finished people. We recruit them while they are still active students and let the pack raise them.

The principle

A compact company of A-players cannot afford to import the wrong culture through senior hires. So we build our own. We take in students, guide them through an absorbing phase inside real work, and offer the honest promise of a full seat if they earn it. The guides are the founders and the team — reinforced by doctors, university teachers, and professionals on temporary contract. Where a student needs an outside course or school to be ready, we arrange it. In time, the ones who graduate become the guides, and run the next Academy themselves.

Scholar

University level — the engineering, software, design, and commercial future.

Recruited from Bandung's institutions while still studying. Absorbs alongside the Studio, Cerebellum, and The Hunt. Graduates into the senior technical and commercial seats.

Cadet

Upper-secondary level — the craft and making future.

Recruited from SMA. Absorbs alongside The Den and Wolver Industry under the craft leads. Graduates into the trades that build every product by hand.

The three-step journey

Orientation — Absorb

Join while still a student. Stand inside the real work, watch, and take in how the pack thinks and builds. Guided by the founders, the team, and contracted specialists.

Contribution — Own a piece

Take real responsibility on real products under a senior. Any outside course or schooling needed to be ready is arranged for them.

Initiative — Graduate & join

Graduate and officially join the pack in a full seat. In the next phase, become the senior who guides the generation behind you.

Selection stays taste-first

We select for taste and character before credentials, in stages, so the filter protects the culture as much as it fills the seats. A brilliant student who would dilute the pack is still a no.
