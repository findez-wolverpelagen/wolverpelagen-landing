The name

Where the word comes from.

Wolverpelagén is a constructed word — deliberate, layered, specific. It was not named after a place or a person. It was named after a territory and a nature. The name has two roots.

Wolver
from wolf — Canis lupus
The apex predator of open terrain. Not the loudest animal, not the largest — the most intelligent, the most coordinated, the most relentless in pursuit. The wolf hunts in packs: each member reads the terrain, the conditions, and the others at once, and acts. That is exactly what we build — systems that read, coordinate, and act.
é meets at
pelagén
from pelagus — the deep open ocean
Latin and Greek for the deep open sea. From the same root comes the word that defines this nation: archi-pelago — the chief sea, the principal ocean expanse. Indonesia is the world's largest archipelago. Wolverpelagén carries Indonesia's geographic identity and global ocean ambition in a single syllable.
é

The accent on the é — written in the French style — gives the word an international character while staying rooted in classical language. A global ambition, built from an Indonesian foundation, carrying the ocean that defines this nation — in one mark.

Wolverpelagén — the wolf of the deep open ocean. The wolf of the archipelago. The intelligence that belongs in the most demanding environment on earth.

The animal as a design principle

The wolf was not chosen because it is fierce.

Many animals are fierce. The wolf was chosen for how it operates — and that is exactly how Wolverpelagén builds its products. Each behaviour of the animal maps to a behaviour of the system.

The wolf reads the terrain continuously.
Our products read the operator's environment continuously — impact, load, fatigue, vessel motion. Always on, always logging. The system never stops paying attention.
The wolf coordinates the pack without a visible signal.
The intelligence layer connects every seat, every sensor, every data point into a single picture. The commander sees the whole pack — not individual readings.
The wolf does not announce the hunt.
The system works quietly. It does not interrupt the operator; it surfaces information only when it matters. Precision over noise.
The wolf is built for endurance, not just speed.
Wolver One is not designed for one mission. It is designed for years of service in the hardest conditions on earth — saltwater, heat, shock, and time.
The pack gets smarter with every hunt.
Every mission logged trains the intelligence. Every unit deployed adds to the network. The system that exists in five years is built on data from the first deployment.

Visual identity

The palette is the wolf.

Every colour exists in the grey wolf — not as decoration, but as a design principle. The current system distils the coat to its essentials: deep ink, a warm seam of light, and the gaze.

Ink
#181816
The undercoat. Primary ground and primary authority. Everything is built on it.
Wolf Grey
#b8b5b0
The body fur in motion. Secondary text, labels, and quiet structure.

The warm edge along the guard hairs. Seams, ticks, and fine structure.
Muzzle Paper
#f2efe8
Underbelly and muzzle. Light ground, breathing room, contrast against the ink.

Clean, cut contrast. Headlines and the sharpest edges against the dark.
Wolf's Eye Gold

THE accent. The gaze. Used sparingly — this is what people remember.

The rule of the gold: use the Wolf's Eye where you need the reader to stop and remember. Not as decoration — as a signal. When the gold appears, it means: this matters.

Note · This coat supersedes the grey-wolf swatch set of the first edition — same principle, refined to the ink-and-gaze system now used across every Wolverpelagén surface.

The brand in one page

Four answers, if that is all there is time for.

What we are
The intelligence layer of the modern tactical vessel. We build systems that read, record, and learn from the operator's physical environment — starting with the seat.
What we are not
A seat manufacturer. A sensor company. A software startup. We are a platform company that begins with physical products — because that is where the operator is.
Who we build for
The maritime operator. The commander who needs real-time crew status. The institution that cannot afford to lose people to preventable physical damage.
Where we are going
From the seat — to console, storage, deck, gear. From Indonesia — to ASEAN, the Gulf, the world. From one sensor — to an AI mission agent that learns from every deployment.
